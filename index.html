The internet that powers the future without burning the planet

GreenRoute is a decentralised data network powered 100% by solar and wind energy, with zero carbon emissions.

The problem we solve

Data centres consume 1% of all electricity worldwide

As data demand doubles every 4 years, digital infrastructure still depends on fossil fuels. We change that.
