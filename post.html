GreenRoute expands to 12 countries in 2026

Partnerships 5 min read

New green hubs, local energy storage and expansion into remote regions with strong social and environmental impact potential.

Global expansion

In 2026, GreenRoute will expand its presence to 12 countries, reinforcing the network with new green hubs and local energy storage solutions.

The strategy prioritises regions with limited digital infrastructure but strong potential for social and environmental impact. Each new node is designed to operate with renewable energy and low maintenance costs.

Africa
+5 countries in 2026
Latin America
+4 countries in 2026
Asia-Pacific
+3 countries in 2026
Climate commitment

With this expansion, GreenRoute reinforces its commitment to climate neutrality, transparency and digital inclusion in communities that need a fairer and more resilient connection.

"The internet of the future cannot depend on models that degrade the planet."

Each new hub is certified by independent auditors and impact data is published monthly on GreenRoute's open platform.
